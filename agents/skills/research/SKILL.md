# Research

Investigate a topic through local exploration, web research, and reasoning.

## Workflow

### 1. Investigate

Gather information relevant to the topic:

- Load relevant skills
- Search the web for best practices, patterns, and current information
- Read local source code, configs, docs, tests if relevant
- Cast a wide net. Don't assume you know the answer before looking.
- Check opinions on reddit and other review websites

### 2. Synthesize

Organize your findings into whatever format best fits the question.

### 3. Discuss

Present your findings, then:

- Surface open questions or uncertainties
- Flag areas worth exploring further
